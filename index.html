About

A little bit about myself. I am a Purdue University Graduate (Boiler Up!) with a degree in Hospitality and Tourism Management. I have worked in the hotel industry for the past decade. I currently am a Sales Account Manager that oversees the business travel, athletic, association, fraternal, medical and entertainment markets for a full service 263 guestroom hotel. I have always enjoyed and am passionate about collaborating with others to develop the best possible solutions for my clients. I thrive on communication, have great time management skills and adjust to various situations quickly. Software development is always something I have been interested in and 2020 is the year I decided to take the leap and immerse myself in all things coding. I have thoroughly enjoyed this as my skillset aligns extremely well. As the saying goes, time really does fly by when you are having fun.
